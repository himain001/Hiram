Vision & Development Agenda

The Ngobit Vision Fund focuses on sustainable, people-driven development. Key priorities include:

  • Youth Empowerment: create training programs for digital skills, agribusiness, and trade.
  • Education Support: improve learning resources, bursaries, and mentorship networks.
  • Water & Sanitation: extend access to safe water sources and maintain local boreholes.
  • Health & Wellness: organize outreach clinics and health-awareness campaigns.
  • Infrastructure & Environment: champion better roads, lighting, and tree-planting initiatives.
  • Transparency & Accountability: promote open communication and responsible resource use.
